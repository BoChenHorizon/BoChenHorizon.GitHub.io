  Professional Affiliations

I started my academic career as an Assistant Professor in 1982 at Zhejiang University (China). After obtaining my Ph.D., I joined the faculty of the University of Warwick (UK) in 1994. I sit on the Management Board of the Centre for Discrete Mathematics and its Applications; am Senior Associate of the Centre for Complexity Science.

Other past or current professional affiliations include: Visiting Professor of Stanford University; Chair Professor of Tsinghua University; Guest Professor of Zhejiang University; Tai-Shan Scholar Chair Professor of Shandong Province, China; Visiting Professor of University of Cambridge; Chair Professor of Fudan University.
